Mahatma Gandhi Sarvodaya Sangh

Dr. Manibhai Desai, a devoted follower of Mahatma Gandhi, came along with Gandhiji to the village of Uruli Kanchan and chose to stay there permanently. Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi‚Äôs thoughts and dedicated to rural development, Dr. Manibhai Desai established the Mahatma Gandhi Sarvodaya Sangh in 1950 at Uruli Kanchan. The primary objective of this organization is to work for the overall development of people living in rural areas. As a part of this mission, and to ensure educational facilities for students in Uruli Kanchan and nearby villages, the institution was formally registered in 1952. The organization, committed to rural development, education, healthcare, and self-reliance, has made significant contributions in the field of education. In 1952, it established Mahatma Gandhi Vidyalaya. In 1976, Mahatma Gandhi Junior College was founded, and in 2002, Padmashri Manibhai Desai College was established. The institution also founded Dr. Cyrus Poonawalla English Medium School in 2010, and earlier in 2000, the B. G. Shirke Pre-Primary School and Mahatma Gandhi Primary School were established. More than eight thousand students are currently studying in this educational campus, and the institution has educated several generations over the years. Many of its students have made remarkable achievements even at the national level. In recognition of Dr. Manibhai Desai‚Äôs social work, the Government of India honored him with the Padma Shri award in 1968, and in 1982 he was awarded the prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award, considered the Nobel Prize of Asia. He was also honored with the Jamnalal Bajaj Award and several other notable awards. The institution founded by him is celebrating its Amrit Mahotsav in 2027, and the reputation it enjoys today is undoubtedly the result of the dedication and commitment of every individual associated with it.

Padmashri Manibhai Desai Mahavidyalaya

To provide the students of Uruli Kanchan and the surrounding areas with access to higher education within their own locality, Padmashri Manibhai Desai College was established in 2002 in memory of Padmashri Dr. Manibhai Desai, the founder of the Mahatma Gandhi Sarvodaya Sangh. This is the only college in the region that offers undergraduate courses in Arts, Commerce, Science, and Computer Science, along with postgraduate programs such as M.A. (Marathi), M.A. (History), and M.Com.
